The build loop
that only moves forward on evidence.

Checkpoint → Verify → Rollback: every turn is committed only if it passes tests, lint, a goal-satisfaction critic, and a thrash detector. A structured Continuity Graph remembers decisions, invariants, and past failures across the whole build — not a truncated transcript. Optionally race two coding agents against each other and let a bandit learn which one actually wins at your kind of work.

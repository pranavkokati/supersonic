How it works

Prompt, folder, loop.

Enter an optional prompt, choose a local folder, and send. Every turn is committed only if it earns it — the live view shows the checkpoint timeline and the Continuity Graph filling in as it works.

  1. 1
    Prompt — describe what to build, or leave it blank and Supersonic grounds an idea.
  2. 2
    Folder — choose where the project should live locally.
  3. 3
    Send — it plans, builds, verifies, checkpoints or rolls back, and ships to GitHub.
